Photographer - Videographer

Sara Kirschner is a photographer and videographer based out of New Jersey. Studying at Syracuse University, Sara is pursuing a dual degree in Television, Radio & Film and Information Management & Technology with a Fashion & Beauty Communications Milestone from the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communicaitons and School of Information Studies.

On campus Sara works as a photographer and videographer for Moody Magazine and Zipped Magazine, as well as is the Head of Photography and Videography for Mixtape Magazine. Sara is also a Creative Intern for the Syraucse University Athletics Department and Head of Fiscal for the Fashion and Design Society.

The summer of 2021, Sara lived in Haifa, Israel and worked in the heart of Tel Aviv as the head of PR for Couture TLV - House for Indepent Designers.
