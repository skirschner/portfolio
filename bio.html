Photographer - Videographer

Sara Kirschner is a photographer and videographer whose work focuses on representation and showcasing people's stories. As a girl who didn't hear her story until her freshman year of college, she believes no one should have to wait that long to see or meet someone like them.

Graduated from Syracuse University S.I. Newhouse School for Public Communications and School for Information Studies, Sara Kirschner continually explores advancements in techology and data to push the bounds of traditional media.

Sara Kirschner has expereince operating as the Head of Photography and Videography for Mixtape Magazine as well as the primary PR Manager for a couture house for independent designers. With work covering a variety of mediums, Sara has pieces showcased by a variety of organizations and publications.
